Common Inquiries

What is the intake process? expand_more

Our intake process begins with a comprehensive RehabAtlas assessment. Once cleared, patients undergo a 48-hour medical stabilization period in our on-site detox wing before transitioning to their private suite.

Are families involved in the program? expand_more

Yes, we offer a dedicated Family Healing Weekend once a month, conducted virtually or in-person at our Carmel annex, focused on systemic recovery and setting boundaries.

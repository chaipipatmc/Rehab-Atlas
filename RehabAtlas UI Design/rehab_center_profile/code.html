The Cedars Recovery Institute

Holistic Approach Trauma-Informed Care Dual Diagnosis

Nestled within thirty acres of secluded woodland, The Cedars provides a tranquil environment designed for profound healing. Our program is built on the premise that recovery is not just the absence of illness, but the presence of meaning and connection.

We limit our occupancy to ensure that every individual receives the personalized attention required for a transformative journey. Here, clinical excellence meets architectural serenity.

Treatment Philosophy

Our methodology blends evidence-based clinical practices with alternative therapies. We believe in treating the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—rather than just the symptoms of addiction.
